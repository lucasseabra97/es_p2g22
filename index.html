About Us

Sistema composto por um conjunto de dashboards e gráficos ilustrativos com informação relativa à circulação de autocarros na zona metroplitana do Porto sob a tutela da STCP. A informação relativa aos autocarros, como por exemplo a sua rota, velocidade média e localização, será consumida recorrendo à API fornecida pela STCP. Pretende-se mostrar diversas vistas sobre os dados: informação individual relativa a um dado autocarro, informação global relativamente a todos os autocarros em circulação, bem como mapas de densidade. Além disso, após o processamento dos dados, poderemos inferir diversas informações/métricas interessantes relativamente ao estado do trânsito.

Personas

Manuel Silva

Manuel is 21 years old and he is a Computer Science student, currently studying at FCUP. However his location is near S. Joao’s Hospital and as everyday he has to take the bus, the bus is the essential means of transport for him. Over the years leaving in this chaotic routine and subsequente technological evolution, Manuel realized that Porto has a great potential becoming a smart city and therefore invest in several areas such as traffic. Being an expert in the area, Manuel knows that nowadays it’s possible to collect data through access of sensors, and therefore allow the existence of a system that could display all the buses in the city and their traffic. So that he and other students that live like him, away from their university, keep track of their buses without having the trouble of losing it.

Elisa Rodrigues

Elisa is 30 years old and works as a manager in STCP, a company holding a fleet of buses in Porto. Her daily job mainly consists in monitoring the public transportation network. With all the data gathered from the sensors scattered throughout Porto, Elisa is able to analyse traffic information such as the location, speed, destination, among other fields of each bus and monitor the fleet as they are displayed in a real time map. Plus, she can also analyze the data in the form of charts and graphics. That way, she is given a great responsibility since any trouble that may occur in the network must be reported to other bus drivers so they can reroute in time to avoid lets say, an accident.
