Personas

Manuel Silva

Manuel is 21 years old and he is a Computer Science student, currently studying at FCUP. However his location is near S. Joao’s Hospital and as everyday he has to take the bus, the bus is the essential means of transport for him. Over the years leaving in this chaotic routine and subsequente technological evolution, Manuel realized that Porto has a great potential becoming a smart city and therefore invest in several areas such as traffic. Being an expert in the area, Manuel knows that nowadays it’s possible to collect data through access of sensors, and therefore allow the existence of a system that could display all the buses in the city and their traffic. So that he and other students that live like him, away from their university, keep track of their buses without having the trouble of losing it.
